Patient Information Brochure

“For I will restore health to you, and your wounds I will heal, declares the Lord.” – Jeremiah 30:17

In our effort to provide high-quality patient care, we request you to take a few minutes to read this brochure. It provides essential information for patients and attendants during their stay. Thank you for your co-operation.

SERVICES
Nurse Call System (Private Ward)
  • Press the red call button when assistance is needed.
  • Nurses on duty will attend immediately.
Chaplaincy Service
  • 24/7 chaplaincy service available.
  • Contact nurse for prayers or spiritual support.
Canteen Services
  • Food courts available in A Block.
  • Tuck shops available on all floors (snacks, tea, coffee).
  • VGS canteen available 24/7 in F Block.
Dietary Service
  • Available for patients and attendants.
  • Special therapeutic diets available.
  • Dietician consultation via nurses.
  • Timing: 5 am – 9 pm daily.
  • À la carte service on request.
Cloak Room
  • Located on ground floor near A Block entrance.
  • Available for luggage storage.
Ayah (Female Helper)
  • One female attendant allowed with patient.
  • If unavailable, ward nurse will help arrange ayah (standard charges apply).
MUTUAL CO-OPERATION
Wrist Band
  • Must be worn until discharge.
  • Inform nurse if damaged or unclear.
Feedback
  • Share feedback to improve services.
  • WhatsApp: 8300205314
  • Forms available with nurses.
Visitor Pass
  • No pass → No entry.
  • 2 passes issued at admission.
  • Return passes to ward nurse at discharge.
Medicines
  • Administered by nurses as prescribed.
  • Pharmacy available 24/7.
Beds
  • Do not remove side rails.
  • Call nurse before getting in/out of bed.
  • Follow safety instructions.
Linen (Bedsheets / Patient Gown)
  • Changed daily.
  • Inform nurse for replacement if soiled.
  • Blankets and towels available.
PAYMENT PROCESS
Online Payment Portal
  • Use CMC Patient Portal (App/Website).
  • Enables appointment booking.
  • Lab payments.
  • Pharmacy bills.
  • IP bill payments.
Bills, Receipts & Refund
  • Keep all receipts safe.
  • Billing office: Ground floor near MS Office.
  • Weekdays: 8 am – 6 pm.
  • Saturday: 8 am – 2 pm.
Insured & Credit Billed Patients
  • Contact ACCESS/CARE section (A002).
  • ACCESS: Cashless services.
  • CARE: Authorization & reimbursement.
  • Office near MS Office.
  • Timings same as billing.
Bank & ATM
  • SBI Bank – F Block, 1st Floor.
  • SBI ATM – OPD building.
  • Federal ATM – Near A Block entrance.
No Tipping
  • Do not tip staff.
  • Against institutional policy.
TRANSPORTATION
Inter Hospital Transport
  • Free buses between Ranipet & Vellore campuses till 11 pm.
  • Contact travel desk: 04172-224086.
  • Additional travel available via travel desk.
Parking Facility
  • Available for 2-wheelers and 4-wheelers.
In-Hospital Transport
  • Buggy service available within campus.
Ambulance Service
  • Inform nurse for ambulance.
  • Available 24/7 (charges applicable).
LET’S KEEP OUR ENVIRONMENT CLEAN
Cleanliness & Hygiene
  • Do not throw waste outside.
  • Use bins provided.
  • Leave food trays outside after meals.
No Spitting
  • Strictly prohibited.
  • Can spread infection.
Non-Smoking Area
  • Smoking prohibited.
  • No incense sticks or coils.
  • Electric repellents allowed.
Waste Disposal
  • Red: Infectious plastic waste.
  • Yellow: Anatomical/soiled waste.
  • Green: Food waste.
  • Brown: Paper waste.
Noise-Free Environment
  • Keep TV volume low.
  • Cooperate with co-patients.
  • Switch off TV by 10 pm.
